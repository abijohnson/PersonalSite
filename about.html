My name is Abi Johnson and I am a recent graduate from Western Oregon University! I majored in Computer Science and really have enjoyed my stay at Western. I chose Computer Science because I've always been drawn to all things technology ever since I was little. When I was gradutating high school, my mom decided to begin her path in getting her degree in Computer Science! After being exposed to it a little and seeing all the cool things you could do with code, I was hooked. So shortly after graduating high school, I started my own CS path!
Computer Science isn't all I enjoy, I love art and design with a fiery passion. This is why I personally like front-end development the most, because I can mix these two awesome mediums. I also really enjoy cooking, playing video games, cross-stitching, drawing, baking, and exploring nature!
