My name is Abi Johnson and I am a recent graduate from Western Oregon University! I majored in Computer Science and really have enjoyed my stay at Western. I chose Computer Science because I've always been drawn to all things art and technology ever since I was young, and I believe Computer Science lends itself to blending the two mediums together perfectly. I personally strive to always create something that is clean, colorful, and accessible to all different types of users.
Having worked in retail and team management, I have gained a major respect for users and I am very passionate about making sure that their needs are met. I beleive this time working alongside and assisting people who are constantly using new technologies made me have a deeper understanding of what people are truly looking for in the applications they use. Not only that, but I have grown a huge appreciation for a good challenge! My goal in any environment is to learn more and be challenged so that I can be a better programmer. Alongside Computer Science, I also thoroughly enjoy organization, drawing, baking, playing video games, and taking adventures out into nature!
